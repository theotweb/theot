  • Sample of the Text: Judges 1:16-19, 1:27, 2:01-03, 2:15-17, 3:07-08, 3:27-31, 4:01-02, 4:19-21, 5:07-11, 6:05, 6:19-21, 7:01, 7:22, 8:07, 8:26, 9:26, 9:50-51, 11:04-05, 11:29, 12:10-13, 14:03, 14:10-13, 15:12, 16:12-14, 17:05-06, 17:07, 18:07-09, 18:17, 19:29-30, 20:26-28, 21:01-03, 21:11-12, 21:22..
  • Sample Size: 13.38% of the book.
  • Sample of Manuscripts: 38. BL 480 (15th), BL 481 (17th), Cambridge Add. 1570 (1588), Davies Dabra Bizan (16th), Davies, Kebran (1417-1418), Davies, Makelle Octateuch 1 (1409-1410), Davies Maqale Octateuch 2 (15th), EMDA 79 (Mota Giyorgis Church, G1-IV-244, 18th), EMIP 00683 (CFRRC 29, 18th), EMIP 00743 (Chelekot Selasse 2, 18th), EMIP 00754 (Chelekot Selasse 13, l18th), EMIP 1037+1038 (15th), EMIP 01115 (1895), EMIP 02111 (Haverford Octateuch 17-18th), EMML 0039 (19th), EMML 0510 (18th), EMML 1842 (1662), EMML 1929a (1438-1468), EMML 2098 (15th), EMML 2388 (1768), EMML 2436 (1663), EMML 6359 (1678), EMML 6557 (1894), EMML 7637 (16th), EMML 9001 (e15th), Ethio-Spare (‘Addi Qolqwal Giyorgis, 16th), Ethio-Spare (Qaqama Qeddest Maryam Dabra Gannat, 1664), Ethio-Spare (Ura Masqal 40, 14th), Gunda Gunde 60 (16th), Gunda Gunde 88 (15th), IES 0077 (1934), Pistoia Martini etiop. 2 (Bibliotheca Forteguerriana di Pistioa, 1438), UNESCO 02.01 (1847-1901), UNESCO 02.02 (e-20th), UNESCO 02.04 (1900-1925), UNESCO 08.2 (1847-1901), Vatican Cerulli 75 (1938).
  • Number of Data Points Generated: 78,470.
  • EA1 Best Representative – EthioSPaRe Ura Masqal Orit (14th).
  • EA2 Best Representative – Davies Dabra Bizan Orit (16th).
  • A good example of an EA outlier text is Cambridge 1570 (1588).
  • Stan/MTR Best Representative – UNESCO 2.4 (1900-1925).
  • No Minor Recensions: Though BL480 (15th), EMIP 1037_38 (15th), EthioSPaRe Addi Qwolqwol Giyorgis ((16th), Cambridge 1570 (1588), EMML 7637 (16th), Dav Maqale 2A (15th), DavMaqale 2B (15th), and GG 60 (16th), all showed a high degree of difference (up to 24%) from the majority of the tradition and from one another, they do not bear witness to a minor recension.
