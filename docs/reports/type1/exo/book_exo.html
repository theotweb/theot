Stuffs1-goes-here
This study of the Textual History of Ethiopic Exodus was carried out as a pilot project in collaboration with the Tofsi HaOrit project at the University of Tel Aviv, directed by Prof. Dalit Rom-Shiloni. Work was carried out between November 2024 and August 2025 when we delivered a report on our findings at the World Congress of Jewish Studies in Jerusalem 4-8 August 2025. A video of that presentation can be found here.
