Jonah: Sample, Best Representatives, Minor Recensions, Other Studies
  • Sample of the Text: Entire book.
  • Sample Size: 100% of the book.
  • Sample of Manuscripts: 105.
  • Number of Data Points Generated: 148,470.
  • EA1 Best Representative – EthioSPaRe `Ura Qirqos 046 (=UM=C3-IV-71, 14-15th). Cluster 1.
  • EA2 Best Representative – IES 436 (15-16th). Cluster 2.
  • EA3 Best Representative – BN Abb 35 (17th). Cluster 3.
  • EA4 Best Representative – British Museum Orient 496 (17th). Cluster 4.
  • EA6 Best Representative – Ethio-SPaRe RQG-017 (=G4-IV-310, 16-17th). Cluster 6.
  • EA7 Best Representative – EMDA 285 (=G4-IV-71, 20th). Cluster 7.
  • Stan1 Best Representative – EMML 2440 (1663). Cluster 9
  • Stan2 Best Representative – EMIP 2007, Tweed 58 (18th). Cluster 10.
  • Stan3 Best Representative – EMML 6930 (18th). Cluster 11.
  • MTR Best Representative – VatCer 75 (1930). Cluster 12.
  • Minor Recensions: EMIP 949 (e20th), UNESCO 10.34 (1931), and EMML 7942 (20th) represent a minor recension, translated from the Latin Vulgate, with a 40% degree of difference from the rest of the tradition with with only a 2% difference between them. All this work was done by Jeremy R. Brown. See Jonah in the “Ethiopic Biblical Tradition: A Dissertation Submitted to the Faculty of. . .Catholic University of America” (Washington D.C. 2019).
