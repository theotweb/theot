Garry Jost and Steve Delamarter contributed a study on the textual history of the Book of Jeremiah to a conference organized by Martin Heide and Stefan Weninger on Ethiopic Jeremiah. The proceedings were published in "The Digital Methodology of the Textual History of the Ethiopic Old Testament (THEOT) Project Applied to Ethiopic Jeremiah 2." Studies on the Ethiopic Old Testament: Prolegomena to a Critical Edition of the Jeremiah Cycle. Edited by Stefan Weninger and Martin Heide. Aethiopica Supplements, 11. Wiesbaden: Harrassowitz, 2022. Pages 79-106.
